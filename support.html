Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

+

You can track your order by going to the "My Orders" section in your dashboard and clicking on "View Details" for the specific order.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept credit cards, purchase orders, and net banking for all business accounts.

How long does shipping take?

+

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping options are available at checkout for 1-2 business day delivery.

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy for unopened products. Please contact our support team to initiate a return.
